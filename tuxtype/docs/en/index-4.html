4. Practice and Tutorial

Tux Typing has tutorials for each of its game play types as well as a section to practice your typing skills in.

4.1 Key Cascade

The Tutorial will show you the basics of playing Key Cascade.

4.2 Word Cascade

This tutorial will show you the basics of playing Word Cascade.

4.3 Free Typing

Same style of game play as "Key Cascade", except when a fish hits the ground, you do not lose a life.

You can think of this as Tux's own private little holodeck!
